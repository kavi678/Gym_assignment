Arms Excercises




Bench Dips


  • Grip the edge of the bench with your hands, keep your feet together and legs straight.
  • Lower your body straight down.
  • Slowly press back up to the starting point.
  • Make this harder by raising your feet off the floor and adding weight.

Elevated Pike Shoulder Shrug

  • Use a bench or an object to elevated your feet.
  • Slowly lower your body while keeping your elbows locked.
  • Slowly raise your body back to the start position.

Chin-Ups


  • Grab the bar shoulder width apart with a suspinated grip.
  • With your body hanging and arms fully extended, pull yourself up until your chin is the bar.
  • Slowly return to starting position. Repeat.

Diamond Push-Ups


  • Place your hand near to each other firmly to the ground.
  • Flatten your back and slowly lower your body.
  • Draw shoulder blades towards backs and keep elbows tucked to your body.
